What is Call Forwarding Fraud?

Scammers trick you into dialing a code (like *401*number#) that forwards all your incoming calls to their device. This allows them to intercept voice-based OTPs and bypass your security measures without your knowledge.

How to Spot a Fake Message

  • priority_high
    Artificial Sense of Urgency Phrases like "Action Required" or "Account Locked" to force a panic reaction.
  • link_off
    Suspicious Links URLs that look similar but aren't official (e.g., faceb00k.com instead of facebook.com).
  • person_search
    Unknown or Hidden Sender International numbers or random email addresses pretending to be your bank.
  • spellcheck
    Poor Grammar & Formatting Professional organizations rarely send texts with typos or strange punctuation.
  • lock_reset
    Requests for Personal Info Legitimate banks will never ask for your PIN, OTP, or password via text message.
